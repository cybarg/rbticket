More about this event

On July 7th, Las Ventas will host the Grand Slam of FMX for the 16th time.

What Star Wars is for movie fans that is Red Bull X-Fighters and Plaza de Toros de Las Ventas for FMX fans. This amazing bullring in Madrid has been the venue for the "Classic Battle" for the last 16 years and it has witnessed some truly titanic head-to-head fights in its time. Since 2002, the FMX elite has been flying through the air consistently at Las Ventas, one of the world's most famous bullfighting arenas.

In 2015 Pagès proved the impossible is possible with a historic Madrid hat-trick and a year later he was able to be victorious again, setting another record. Will someone be able to stop Tom Pagès in 2017?
